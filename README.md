I am a theoretical, mathematical, and computational physicist. I am an Associate Professor of Physics at Brock University, where I won the FMS Award for Excellence in Teaching. I am also a Sessional Lecturer at McMaster University. My research, supported by the NSERC Discovery Grant, focuses on the nature of time and causality in general relativity and quantum mechanics, as well as symbolic and high-performance scientific computing. In my spare time I am also a multi-instrumentalist and composer.
